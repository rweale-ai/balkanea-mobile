Nea Booking Journey
End-to-end flow: conversational search → hotel evaluation with Nea in context → comparison → booking → trip container. Nea is reachable at every step.
1
Collect & search SEARCH TAB
Nea gathers destination, dates, guests, budget, style, stars conversationally (text or voice), confirms with summary pills, then shows 2–3 hotel cards inline.
2
Hotel Detail HOTEL DETAIL
Photo strip, rating badge, location + distances, date pills, amenities, room options, cancellation policy. Sticky "Select your room" CTA.
Session memory: opening a hotel silently adds it to the "viewed hotels" list Nea can compare later.
3
Ask Nea in context BOTTOM SHEET
Bottom sheet over Hotel Detail (no navigation away). Nea opens with a review summary: score, count, positives, watch-outs.
4
Clarify & compare BOTTOM SHEET
Follow-ups: beach distance, breakfast, couples fit. If other hotels were viewed, a "Compare with…" chip appears → side-by-side comparison card with Nea's verdict.
↺ Back to chat → view another hotel (repeats 2–4, builds comparison set)
5
Room selection ROOM SELECT
All room types: size, guests, meal plan, cancellation, price/night + total for stay. "Nea recommends" banner on best-fit room. "Book this room" per card.
6
Booking form → confirmed BOOKING
Guest details pre-filled when logged in → payment → Booking Confirmed with confirmation code (existing screens, unchanged).
7
Trip container DASHBOARD
Booking becomes a Trip card ("Santorini Trip · Aug 2026"): hotel summary + Add to trip tiles — Flights, Restaurants, Car Rental, Tours, Insurance. Tiles link to Nea or show "Coming soon".
Retention hook: the trip is the container for the whole travel experience — every future vertical plugs in here.
Key patterns
Orange gradient = primary action / user turn
Orange-light = Nea affordances, chips, badges
Dark green = Nea avatar only
Green = confirmed / free cancellation
